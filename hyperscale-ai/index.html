// THE ACTUAL REASON

why does this look better than topaz?

A

HAT sees the whole picture

normal upscalers (RealESRGAN, Topaz default) learn average textures from training data and paste them everywhere. skin becomes porcelain. fabric loses weave. hair clumps.

HAT-GAN uses self-attention — same core idea as GPT, but for pixels. it looks at wide patches and reasons about what belongs there based on context. not averaging. actually thinking.

HAT-GAN Hybrid Attention Transformer
B

stable diffusion without the warping

SD is generative — ask it for texture and it also moves your eyebrows and shifts the lighting. terrible for photos.

the fix: frequency separation. keep the original's low frequencies (your colors, shapes, light). only inject SD's high-frequency micro-texture layer. pores without color drift.

Frequency Separation · ControlNet Tile
C

faces stay human

detects every face before the SD pass. builds a feathered mask around it (+30% padding for hair). blends SD output around faces not on them. GFPGAN v1.4 pass restores eye symmetry and lip shape separately.

GFPGAN v1.4 · Face Detection
D

3 minutes not 30

original pipeline ran SD after the full upscale — huge image, tons of tiles, forever. i figured out SD doesn't care about absolute resolution, just local structure. so now it runs before the final HAT pass at the smaller intermediate size.

same quality. 3 minutes instead of 30. figured that out at 1am.
